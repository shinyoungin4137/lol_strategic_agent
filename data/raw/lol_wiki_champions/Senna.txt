Allies affected by the

.
Obscured units appear as

On the

, obscured champions will be displayed as wraith icons instead of champion icons to the enemy team.
In-game, the 'Cloak of Mist' buff grants camouflage and the 'Wraith Form' buff obscures the unit and grants movement speed. However, 'Cloak of Mist' has the unspecified effect of negating the obscuring effects of 'Wraith Form' (i.e. the unit is not a wraith while inside the mist); the unit has the bonus movement speed even when they are not a wraith (e.g. inside the mist or an enemy is nearby); and 'Wraith Form' also grants the functionality of camouflage (i.e. the unit is treated as a camouflaged unit with regards to
True Sight
,
Control Wards
, and enemy
champions

Given this overly complex interaction between the two buffs granted as well as the overlapping effects, it is easier to describe the effects as a single buff that is modified in specific circumstances rather than trying to establish a new game mechanic.
Senna
likewise has her aura buff and the 'Wraith Form' buff, but her 'Wraith Form' does not share gameplay functionality with other allies benefiting from Wraith Form. For
Senna

Tips & Tricks
Because of its duration,
Curse of the Black Mist
can be utilized in conjunction with
Homeguard
when exiting the base, and importantly early in the lane.
This is at the cost of mana, some of which will regenerate during the walk. Mana is consumed upon finishing the cast, so alternatively the caster can remain in base to regenerate some or all of the amount incurred, though delaying the journey back to lane.
To maximize the distance covered, due to

The haste and stealth combination can be highly effective in initiating ambushes from

Attack speed ratio
The
Attack speed ratio (AS ratio)
adjusts the effectiveness of bonus AS from all sources. It is usually equal to the unit's base AS (level 1 with no bonuses), but tuning to a higher or lower relative value translates to more or less bonus AS received compared to other units with the same base AS.
Total AS = Base AS + Bonus AS ×

The ingame stats HUD displays the
AS ratio
instead of base AS to communicate this comparative effectiveness. The Wiki opts to always list both stats and show the
AS ratio
as "N/A" if the two are equal.
