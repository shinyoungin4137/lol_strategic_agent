Proc damage
Proc damage
is damage type assigned to most
on-hit
effects. It is worth noting that many seeming on-hit effects are actually classified as
ability damage
and so will trigger
ability effects

Proc damage
does not interact with most other effects, such as

or
Arcane Comet

Omnivamp
and
Physical vamp
(where applicable) heal for a percentage of damage dealt.
Effects triggered by damage subtypes (
physical

magic

true

Ability effects
Spell effects
is an umbrella term for effects that trigger whenever a champion deals
ability damage
, including abilities that otherwise behave like basic attacks. Most spell effects deal
proc damage
or
default damage
, which are non-reactive (= the effects don't trigger more effects).
Some spell effects are only triggered by
abilities
, while others may be triggered by any source of
ability damage
.
The basic attack counterpart to
spell effects
are
on-hit
effects, which apply on instances of
basic damage
.
