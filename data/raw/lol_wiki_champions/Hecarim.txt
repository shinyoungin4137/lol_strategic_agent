Attack speed ratio
The
Attack speed ratio (AS ratio)
adjusts the effectiveness of bonus AS from all sources. It is usually equal to the unit's base AS (level 1 with no bonuses), but tuning to a higher or lower relative value translates to more or less bonus AS received compared to other units with the same base AS.
Total AS = Base AS + Bonus AS ×
AS Ratio
(All values in decimal form)
The ingame stats HUD displays the

as "N/A" if the two are equal.

is a mechanic that will result in a champion's attack timer being reset to zero. When this effect takes place during the basic attack's cooldown and not its windup, it allows the attacker to start a second attack quicker than normal.
Casting certain ability and item actives can reset the attack timer.
Hail of Blades
generates an additional
stack
of its effect each time the user resets their attack timer from
attack resets
.
League of Legends content
Champion ability
A
champion ability
(often just
ability
) is a unique action performed by and only available to a specific
champion
. All of these special moves available to a champion collectively constitute their
kit
. A skill point is earned each time the champion
levels up
, which the player can spend on most abilities to learn them and rank them up to improve their potency.
Champion abilities
can have either or both
active
and
passive
effects:
Active effects are
cast
by using their respective
hotkey
and selecting a valid target. Most abilities require spending an amount of
resource
, such as
mana
.
Passive effects may simply be permanently available or are
cast
by fulfilling a requirement or using a specific command.
Most abilities incur a
cooldown
after being cast, during which they cannot be used. This cooldown time is usually reduced by
ability haste
.
Spell effects
may trigger only when a valid ability deals damage. On-cast effects trigger upon an ability's
cast time
completing.

Spell damage
Spell damage
is the damage type dealt by most
abilities
and some
items
and
runes
that affect targets directly, usually a single one.
Spell damage
applies
spell effects
by default.
Arcane Comet's
remaining
cooldown
is reduced by 20% upon dealing an instance of
spell damage
to a champion.
Effects triggered by damage subtypes (
physical
,
magic
,
true
) will always occur regardless of damage type (e.g.
Black Cleaver's
Carve stacking on instances of
physical damage
and
Morellonomicon's
Grievous Wounds
application on instances of
magic damage
).

Stacks multiplicatively
An effect that
stacks multiplicatively
can be calculated by multiplying the effects together.
Most
champion statistics
that stack this way are expressed through percentages.

The following stats
stack multiplicatively
:
Attack speed reduction
Damage modifiers
Percent armor penetration and reduction
Percent magic penetration and reduction
Slow potency reduction
