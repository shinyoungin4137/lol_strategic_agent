Attack speed ratio
The
Attack speed ratio (AS ratio)
adjusts the effectiveness of bonus AS from all sources. It is usually equal to the unit's base AS (level 1 with no bonuses), but tuning to a higher or lower relative value translates to more or less bonus AS received compared to other units with the same base AS.
Total AS = Base AS + Bonus AS ×
AS Ratio
(All values in decimal form)
The ingame stats HUD displays the
AS ratio
instead of base AS to communicate this comparative effectiveness. The Wiki opts to always list both stats and show the
AS ratio
as "N/A" if the two are equal.

Basic attack
The

(or "auto") is the default means by which a unit deals damage, usually equaling
100% AD

.
Basic attacks
are normally unit-targeted

The caster must stand still to complete a brief

before launching a

. Depending on the caster:
If a caster's attacks are non-projectile (e.g.

) launching the attack deals damage immediately.
If a caster's attacks are

, launching the attack sends a projectile that follows the target and deals damage upon striking them.
The caster then incurs a brief cooldown, based on their

. Basic attacks' damage may trigger
on-hit effects
only upon dealing damage.
On-attack
effects will always trigger upon the attack's
windup
completing.
