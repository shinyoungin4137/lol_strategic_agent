The
Attack speed ratio (AS ratio)
adjusts the effectiveness of bonus AS from all sources. It is usually equal to the unit's base AS (level 1 with no bonuses), but tuning to a higher or lower relative value translates to more or less bonus AS received compared to other units with the same base AS.
Total AS = Base AS + Bonus AS ×

(All values in decimal form)
The ingame stats HUD displays the

as "N/A" if the two are equal.

Basic attack
The
basic attack
(or "auto") is the default means by which a unit deals damage, usually equaling
100% AD
physical damage
.
Basic attacks
are normally unit-targeted
edge range
spells, and the
attack range
stat determines their reach.
The caster must stand still to complete a brief
windup
before launching a
basic attack
. Depending on the caster:
If a caster's attacks are non-projectile (e.g.
melee
) launching the attack deals damage immediately.
If a caster's attacks are
projectile
, launching the attack sends a projectile that follows the target and deals damage upon striking them.
The caster then incurs a brief cooldown, based on their
attack speed
. Basic attacks' damage may trigger
on-hit effects
only upon dealing damage.
On-attack
effects will always trigger upon the attack's
windup
completing.

Champion ability
A
champion ability
(often just
ability
) is a unique action performed by and only available to a specific
champion
. All of these special moves available to a champion collectively constitute their
kit
. A skill point is earned each time the champion
levels up
, which the player can spend on most abilities to learn them and rank them up to improve their potency.
Champion abilities
can have either or both
active
and
passive
effects:
Active effects are
cast
by using their respective
hotkey
and selecting a valid target. Most abilities require spending an amount of
resource
, such as
mana
.
Passive effects may simply be permanently available or are
cast
by fulfilling a requirement or using a specific command.
Most abilities incur a
cooldown
after being cast, during which they cannot be used. This cooldown time is usually reduced by
ability haste
.
Spell effects
may trigger only when a valid ability deals damage. On-cast effects trigger upon an ability's
cast time
completing.

Slow
A unit that is
slowed
has reduced
movement speed
for the duration.
Slows
based on a percentage value cannot reduce a target below
110 movement speed
. Some
slows
will instead modify the target's
movement speed
to a static value, which may not be further reduced nor increased.
Only the slow with the highest value is taken into movement speed calculations. All additional slows are ignored until the higher-valued slow wears off. The strength of slows may amplify or decay over their duration. Slows of the same debuff cannot stack and will instead refresh on each application unless otherwise stated.
Negative
slow
values will increase the unit's
movement speed
instead.
Interrupts:
Does not interrupt any actions.
Reduction:
The
duration
is affected by
Tenacity
.
The
strength
is reduced by
slow resist
.
Resist:
Resisted by
crowd control immunity
and
slow immunity
.
Removal:
Removed by
Cleanses
,
Garen's
Decisive Strike
,
Master Yi's
Highlander
and
Nunu's
Biggest Snowball Ever!
.

Charm

summoner spells
Flash
,
Teleport
,

, and
Mark
/
Dash
. All
charms
also apply a
slow
.
Charm
is an
immobilizing
effect
.
Forced action effects
cannot
force the unit to perform an action that is disabled.
Interrupts:
Interrupts
attack windups
(excluding
uncancellable windups
) and all
channeled and charged
abilities, as well as overrides previous
Forced Action
effects.
Reduction:
The
duration
is affected by
Tenacity
.
Resist:
Resisted by
crowd control immunity
and
Dr. Mundo's
Goes Where He Pleases
.
Removal:
Removed by
Cleanses
or when the
Source

Crowd control applied by one's self or an ally cannot be countered in any way, neither
reduced
in duration,

The crowd control effect may still be
cleansed
