The first stack of
Break the Mold
that is applied to a target will snapshot the targets current
armor
and
magic resistance
. All subsequent stacks will reduce the targets resistances based on the snapshotted amount, even if the target's total resistances change. This snapshot lasts until the debuff expires.
For example, applying a stack of
Break the Mold
to a champion with 100
armor
and
magic resistance
will reduce their resistance by 3 and grant
Rell
the lost stats. If their resistances are then boosted to 500 before another stack is applied, the subsequent stack will only reduce their resistance by a further 3 despite 3% of 500 being 15.
Another example, when a target has temporarily gained increased resistances before
Rell
applies the first stack of
Break the Mold
then the snapshot of the target's stats will be of the increased amount. This can result in targets losing significantly more resistances after the duration of the increase ends, for as long as the stacks don't fall off.
If a target has 100 base resistances, and they are increased to 500 temporarily before
Break the Mold
is applied, then the target will lose a total of 15 resistances, up to a total of 75 resistances at 5 stacks, and when the target's resistance buff ends they would only have 25 remaining.

Attack speed ratio
The
Attack speed ratio (AS ratio)
adjusts the effectiveness of bonus AS from all sources. It is usually equal to the unit's base AS (level 1 with no bonuses), but tuning to a higher or lower relative value translates to more or less bonus AS received compared to other units with the same base AS.
Total AS = Base AS + Bonus AS ×
AS Ratio
(All values in decimal form)
The ingame stats HUD displays the
AS ratio
instead of base AS to communicate this comparative effectiveness. The Wiki opts to always list both stats and show the
AS ratio
as "N/A" if the two are equal.

generates an additional
stack
of its effect each time the user resets their attack timer from
