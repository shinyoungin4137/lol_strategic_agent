Attack speed ratio
The
Attack speed ratio (AS ratio)
adjusts the effectiveness of bonus AS from all sources. It is usually equal to the unit's base AS (level 1 with no bonuses), but tuning to a higher or lower relative value translates to more or less bonus AS received compared to other units with the same base AS.
Total AS = Base AS + Bonus AS ×
AS Ratio
(All values in decimal form)
The ingame stats HUD displays the
AS ratio
instead of base AS to communicate this comparative effectiveness. The Wiki opts to always list both stats and show the
AS ratio
as "N/A" if the two are equal.

A
champion ability
(often just
ability
) is a unique action performed by and only available to a specific
champion
. All of these special moves available to a champion collectively constitute their
kit
. A skill point is earned each time the champion
levels up
, which the player can spend on most abilities to learn them and rank them up to improve their potency.
Champion abilities
can have either or both
active
and
passive
effects:
Active effects are
cast
by using their respective
hotkey
and selecting a valid target. Most abilities require spending an amount of
resource
, such as
mana
.
Passive effects may simply be permanently available or are
cast
by fulfilling a requirement or using a specific command.
Most abilities incur a
cooldown
after being cast, during which they cannot be used. This cooldown time is usually reduced by
ability haste
.
Spell effects
may trigger only when a valid ability deals damage. On-cast effects trigger upon an ability's
cast time
completing.

Monster
Monsters

League of Legends

Minion
Minions
are autonomous units that spawn periodically from their nexus and advance along the lanes toward the enemy nexus. They
basic attack
any enemy unit or structure they find in their way, but can be killed for
gold
.
There are four kind of

melee minions
,
caster minions
,

.

Stack
A
stack
refers to a
buff
or
debuff
that can be increased in count under certain circumstances. Stacking effects with durations will refresh per instance, unless otherwise stated.
Stacking effects that time out may cause all stacks to expire at once or one by one in given intervals, depending on the source.

Death
Death
occurs when a unit's
health
is reduced to zero, thereby being killed. The unit can no longer act and is removed or becomes a corpse.
On player death, they can view the
death recap
and the
shop
to purchase items, and some
item actives
can be used while dead.

Arena
Arena
is a team deathmatch game mode organized in a tournament format that consists of eight teams of two players fighting on six different battlefields. Teams brawl in rotating combat rounds while powering themselves up with unique Augments and Items and using multiple environmental features to their advantage, in a display of both deeply tactical and highly explosive combat until the last team standing.

Centered range
Centered range
is calculated from the
center
of a source unit's hitbox to the
center
of a target unit's hitbox.
Centered range is used for most
unit-targeted
abilities.
It is
less
effective and may seem to have diminished reach against
large
units.

Persistent damage
Persistent damage
is the damage type dealt by most damage-over-time (DoT)
abilities
.
Persistent damage
applies
spell effects
by default.
Arcane Comet's
remaining
cooldown
is reduced by 5% upon dealing an instance of
persistent damage
to a champion.
Effects triggered by damage subtypes (
physical
,
magic
,
true
) will always occur regardless of damage type (e.g.
Black Cleaver's
Carve stacking on instances of
physical damage
and
Morellonomicon's
Grievous Wounds
application on instances of
magic damage
).
